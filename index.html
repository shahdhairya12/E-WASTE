About E-Waste

E-waste refers to electronic products that have become unwanted, obsolete, or reached the end of their useful life.

Examples of e-waste include computers, monitors, televisions, mobile phones, and other electronic devices.

Recycling Options

Proper recycling of e-waste is essential to reduce the environmental impact and recover valuable materials.

Many recycling centers and electronic retailers offer e-waste recycling programs. Check with your local authorities for options available in your area.
